About

Join the Think with Earth Ideathon

Turn your ideas into action for a sustainable future. The Think with Earth Ideathon brings together innovators to solve global challenges using technology and creativity.

Register Now

Why Join?

Collaborate with passionate thinkers to tackle issues like climate change and resource efficiency. Whether you're a coder, designer, or idea generator, your contribution matters.

  • Work with diverse teams to create impactful solutions
  • Get guidance from industry mentors
  • Pitch your ideas for a chance to win prizes
  • Connect with sustainability leaders

Key Challenges

Focus on real-world sustainability problems:

  • Renewable Energy: Innovate for cleaner energy systems
  • Circular Economy: Reduce waste, reuse resources
  • Smart Cities: Build sustainable urban solutions
  • Environmental Impact: Monitor and protect ecosystems

How It Works

Form or join a team, brainstorm solutions, and pitch your ideas in a fast-paced, collaborative event. No experience required—just a passion for change.

Our Partners

We're proud to collaborate with leading organizations committed to environmental sustainability and innovation. These partnerships enable us to provide participants with valuable resources, industry insights, and implementation pathways.

Challenge Tracks

TradeApollo

Connecting global energy markets

Develop code to integrate global energy market data, including: Data Sourcing, Web Crawling, Visualization, Integration of multiple data sources!

Unified Power Market: UPM

Connecting global energy markets

Development of an API aimed at gathering and standardizing information for global energy trading. Help us to facilitate energy exchange worldwide by establishing uniform standards!

Global Energy Connect

Connecting global energy markets

Implementation of the approach to inspire the market participants of the energy markets worldwide for the idea of being linked in a global API. Help us devise a strategy that convinces market participants to price data to connect energy markets globally, piece by piece!

CRISP-X

Driving climate resiliency

Siemens CRISP-X: Climate Resilient Infrastructure for Sustainability and Performance Excellence. Join Siemens CRISP-X and harness the power of AI to strengthen infrastructure against climate risks. Our pioneering software analyzes open-source data and employs machine learning to create tailored, location-specific resilience strategies. By integrating climate models and GIS data, we assess risks at the zip code level, predict physical and financial impact on buildings and occupants, thereafter, crafting customized action plans that provide clear roadmaps towards driving climate resiliency. Shape tomorrow with us, where your expertise drives meaningful change!

CLARITY

Driving climate resiliency

We are seeking a proficient individual to collaborate on the development of an interactive and informative map application designed to track climate events. The role involves incorporating a machine learning model for predicting upcoming events. Additionally, the selected candidate will be responsible for establishing linkages between these events and relevant mitigation and adaptation strategies sourced from various data repositories. We are looking for a professional with expertise in spatial data, mapping technologies, and machine learning to contribute to this impactful project.

B3C (Bio-Based Balises Casing)

Ecodesign for train's balises

B3C is a novel idea for transforming balises casing with cardanol resin, an emerging sustainable material. If you're familiar with polymer design, materials testing, or sustainability, that's a good sign that you should join us!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

+

If you have a brain, a heartbeat, and at least one mildly questionable idea at 2 AM—you're in!

Is there a registration fee?

+

Nope! Zero. Zilch. Nada. It's so free, even your wallet will high-five you.

What team size is recommended?

+

We recommend 2 to 4 brains per idea—enough to spark genius, but not so many that you need a project manager to order pizza.

What should teams prepare beforehand?

+

🧠 Some research on sustainability challenges – So your idea doesn't involve solving global warming with ice cubes.
🧰 Brainstorming tools – Sticky notes, whiteboards, AI, carrier pigeons—whatever fuels your creativity.
😄 A positive attitude – Because every great idea starts with "What if…?" and a lot of snacks.

Are there prizes for winners?

+

Oh yes. There will be prizes. 🎁
Think glory, goodies, and maybe even something shiny for your shelf. 🏆
